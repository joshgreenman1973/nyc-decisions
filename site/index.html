The Rest of the Record

A searchable microscope on NYC public records.

Every year, New York City and State agencies generate a vast paper trail beyond the headlines — adjudications, audits, complaints, dispositions, penalty recommendations, oversight reports — public records that nonetheless live scattered across dozens of incompatible PDFs, portals, and archives. The Rest of the Record indexes them in one place. Currently covers OATH (both Trials and Hearings), CCRB complaints and penalties, COIB, the Commission on Human Rights, DOI, the Comptroller, the Public Advocate, TLC Chairperson reviews, DCWP final decisions, and PERB labor decisions. We link out to every original document — see methodology for coverage caps, gaps, and known limits.
